Code Breach is a “Extraction” First Person Shooter with RPG’s components

Engage in a world which the gods left behind

Cruel experiments have left areas of the earth uninhabitable and teeming with ferocious creatures, and it is your duty to clean what is left behind.

Complete missions and shape your destiny

Discover the secrets awaiting you in the exclusion zone.

Go alone or in groups

Do not trust anyone, only your survival instincts. You are alone in this world.
